How It Works ?

  • Nursery Management
    • Create/modify classes
    • Assign each kid to his class
    • Add/remove Teacher
    • Add kid profile and Mobile numbers of parents (to can access the app)
    • Adding daily channel-book for each kid (breakfast, lunch, snacks, Comments and Attachments)
    • Add/modify weekly meals (breakfast & Lunch & snacks )
    • The nursery is able to add the academic subjects and the daily lessons (classwork & homework)
    • Sending Posts and Newsletter for all the Nursery or for any Specific class (Push Notifications)
    • Add/modify nursery profile and galleries
    • View parents’ reviews and overall nursery rating
    • Show how to use the application via video tutorials
    • Nursery Polices and regulations
  • Parents
    • Parents get notifications for any action on app that belongs to their kids
    • Receive Posts and Newsletter
    • Get daily channel-book about meals and other info
    • Receive attachments and pictures for any task done by to their kid
    • Permission to send any requests and show the replies from nursery (Messaging system)
    • Able to see nursery profile, location and its services
    • Watch tutorials videos of how to use the application
    • Ability to add Rating and reviews about the Nursery
    • View daily Academic lessons (classwork and homework) for their kid
